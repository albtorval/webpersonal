--- title: Orange dress author: Alberto Torrejón Valenzuela date: '2020-12-19' categories: - poemas tags: - Granada - Inglés slug: orange-dress ---

That well known dress
Tied to your skin,
Facing the wind,
Shining in the street.
Orange it was,
Subtle, no bluffing,
Never been so charmed,
Tonight,
I’m the lucky.
